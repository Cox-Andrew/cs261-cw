FAQs

FAQs for Attendees

1. How to join an event

Click on join as a participant on the home page, enter account details or create an account if none exists, enter the event id on the next page and click join.

2. How to provide feedback

Select general feedback tab or one of the other tabs on the top, fill in the feedback form and click submit.

3. How to provide feedback anonymously

On the feedback page, select the checkbox for anonymous, and the form will be submitted anonymously. The host will not be able to know the person who submitted the form.

4. How to edit my feedback response

On the feedback form, click edit submission, fill in the form with changes and submit.

5. How to manage/delete account

Once logged in, click on manage account from the navigation bar to access the manage account page.

FAQs for Hosts

1. How to create a series of events

Once logged in, click on the create series link and you will be directed to the create series of event page.

2. How to view feedback from a series of events

Click on the my series link, select the required series and click on view series feedback.

3. How to delete a series

Once the series is selected, click on the delete series and the series will be deleted.

4. How to create an event

Once a series is created, click on my series, select the required series in which the event needs to be created and then click on create event link.

5. How to view feedback from an event

Click on my series, select the series in which the required event was created, click on my meeting, select the event, and then click on view meeting feedback.

6. How to change questions in the event feedback form

Once the event is selected, click on the manage event and then select the template you wish to modify.

7. How to delete an event

Once the event is selected, click on the delete event button and the event will be deleted.

8. How to manage/delete my account

Select manage account from the homepage displayed after logging in, and you will be directed to the manage account page.
