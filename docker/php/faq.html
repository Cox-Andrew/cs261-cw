FAQs

FAQs for Attendees

1. How to join a meeting

Click on join a meeting on the home page, enter account details or create an account if none exists, enter the meeting id on the next page and click join.

2. How to provide feedback

Select general feedback or comprehensive feedback tab from the top, fill in the feedback form and click submit.

3. How to manage/delete account

Once logged in, click on manage account from the navigation bar to access the manage account page.

FAQs for Hosts

1. How to create a session

Once logged in, click on the create session link and you will be directed to the create session page.

2. How to view feedback from a session

Click on the my sessions link, select the required session and click on view session feedback.

3. How to delete a session

Once the session is selected, click on the delete session and the session will be deleted.

4. How to create a meeting

Once a session is created, click on my sessions, select the required session in which the meeting needs to be created and then click on create meeting link.

5. How to view feedback from a meeting

Click on my sessions, select the session in which the required meeting was created, click on my meetings, select the meeting and then click on view meeting feedback.

6. How to change questions in the meeting feedback form

Once the meeting is selected, click on the manage meeting and then select the template you wish to modify.

7. How to delete a meeting

Once the meeting is selected, click on the delete meeting and the meeting will be deleted.

8. How to manage/delete my account

Select manage account from the homepage displayed after logging in, and you will be directed to the manage account page.
